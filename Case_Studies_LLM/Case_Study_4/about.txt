This folder contains materials exploring Artificial Intelligence (AI) and organizational innovation. 

1. sample_text.txt — Explains how AI is transforming industries such as healthcare, education, entertainment, environment, and space exploration. It also highlights challenges like job automation and ethics in AI.

2. LLM4.ipynb — A Jupyter Notebook demonstrating NLP and Large Language Model (LLM) techniques for analyzing, summarizing, and visualizing AI-related text data.

3. HBR_How_Apple_Is_Organized_For_Innovation.pdf — A Harvard Business Review article analyzing Apple’s unique functional structure where experts lead experts, enabling continuous innovation through collaboration and attention to detail.

Together, these files showcase how AI drives global transformation and how expert-led organizational models, like Apple’s, sustain technological innovation.
